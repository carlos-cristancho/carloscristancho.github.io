About me

Hi, I am Carlos Cristancho, a software developer passionate about object-oriented programming and solving complex problems. I use Agile methodologies such as Scrum and Kanban to manage project development, ensuring that each delivery meets efficiency and scalability standards. I specialize in backend development and in creating desktop and mobile applications, implementing solutions that optimize processes and improve organizational productivity.

My experience in Instrumentation and Industrial Control includes the maintenance of transmitters and safety valves, where I have worked on the installation, configuration, and diagnosis of critical equipment. I have ensured that these devices operate reliably and accurately, contributing to the safety and efficiency of industrial plants. Additionally, I have led preventive and corrective maintenance activities on various industrial control equipment.

I also have extensive experience in developing maintenance plans based on the RCM (Reliability-Centered Maintenance) methodology for safety instrumented systems (SIS) and basic process control systems (BPCS), as well as the RBI (Risk-Based Inspection) methodology applied to safety, relief, and safety-relief valves. Additionally, I have designed and executed maintenance plans for fire protection systems, ensuring operability and compliance with safety regulations in industrial facilities.
