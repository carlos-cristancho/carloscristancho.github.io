Projects

In this section, I present a selection of my most representative works, where I have applied object-oriented programming principles and agile methodologies to ensure that the results not only meet functional requirements but also optimize resources and improve productivity.

My passion for technology has led me to tackle complex problems and design scalable solutions that can be adapted to different environments. I invite you to explore each of these projects, developed to solve technical challenges in a creative and effective manner.

  • EngineFourierAnalyzer

    Is a project designed to analyze signals and decompose them into their frequency components using the FFTW library and C++. This software aims to be an efficient and flexible tool for performing Fourier analysis in industrial applications, with a particular focus on fault detection in rotating machinery and predictive maintenance.

  • FolderSyncTask

    Is a Python-based project designed to synchronize the contents of two directories, source and replica, by comparing files and subdirectories. The program ensures that any new files, modified files, or deleted files in the source folder are accurately reflected in the replica folder, maintaining consistency between both directories.

  • AgileTrackSystem

    A project management tool built to facilitate Agile workflows. AgileTrackSystem helps users track project progress, manage tasks, and improve team collaboration by implementing key Agile principles.

  • TaskManagerApp

    Task manager using Python with SCRUM and Kanban methodologies.

About me

Hi, I am Carlos Cristancho, a software developer passionate about object-oriented programming and solving complex problems. I use Agile methodologies such as Scrum and Kanban to manage project development, ensuring that each delivery meets efficiency and scalability standards. I specialize in backend development and in creating desktop and mobile applications, implementing solutions that optimize processes and improve organizational productivity.

My experience in Instrumentation and Industrial Control includes the maintenance of transmitters and safety valves, where I have worked on the installation, configuration, and diagnosis of critical equipment. I have ensured that these devices operate reliably and accurately, contributing to the safety and efficiency of industrial plants. Additionally, I have led preventive and corrective maintenance activities on various industrial control equipment.

I also have extensive experience in developing maintenance plans based on the RCM (Reliability-Centered Maintenance) methodology for safety instrumented systems (SIS) and basic process control systems (BPCS), as well as the RBI (Risk-Based Inspection) methodology applied to safety, relief, and safety-relief valves. Additionally, I have designed and executed maintenance plans for fire protection systems, ensuring operability and compliance with safety regulations in industrial facilities.
